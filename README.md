# EthiScore AI – Explainable AI Credit Intelligence Platform

EthiScore AI is an AI-powered decision-support platform that aims to improve access to finance for Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs), startups, and entrepreneurs by assisting financial institutions with transparent, data-driven credit assessments.

Traditional lending often depends on collateral, formal credit history, and extensive financial documentation. Many promising entrepreneurs, particularly first-time business owners, women-led enterprises, youth entrepreneurs, and businesses operating in emerging markets, may lack these traditional indicators despite running sustainable businesses. This can make it difficult for lenders to assess creditworthiness while managing financial risk.

EthiScore AI addresses this challenge by combining Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Explainable AI (XAI) to analyze financial and business information and generate actionable credit intelligence. Instead of replacing human decision-makers, the platform provides transparent recommendations that help loan officers make more informed lending decisions.

# Project Status

EthiScore AI is currently being developed as a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for the Cursor AI Hackathon Ethiopia. The initial version focuses on demonstrating how explainable AI can support responsible lending decisions while improving financial inclusion for underserved businesses.
